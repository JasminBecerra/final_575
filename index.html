Chicago Public High Schools: College Readiness

The state of Chicago Public Schools (CPS) has garnered attention in discussions on inequality in American public education. In early 2017, Mayor Rahm Emanuel proposed a new set of graduation requirements for CPS high school seniors. In particular, one additional requirement for high school seniors has resulted in backlash from the public:

proof of acceptance into college or the military, or a trade or a ‘gap-year’ program

So, what does college readiness look like in Chicago?
